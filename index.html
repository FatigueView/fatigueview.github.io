What is FatigueView?
FatigueView is a new large-scale dataset for vision-based drowsiness detection, which is constructed for the research community towards closing the data gap behind the industry.

2021/4/25
FatigueView is officially released during Auto Shanghai 2021.
Advantages
Diversity
Both RGB and infrared (IR) cameras at five different positions.
Practical
More than1,600,000 drowsiness visual signs from subtle to obvious.
Richness
Hierarchical annotations (element, set and event) and 16 baselines.
